About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Navdeep Kaur

With over two decades of dedicated practice, Dr. Navdeep Kaur is one of Patiala's most sought-after dermatologists — combining precision diagnostics with a warm, empathetic approach.

She holds an MBBS (2004) and an MD in Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy (2008) from the prestigious Dayanand Medical College & Hospital, Ludhiana. As a Senior Consultant at Manipal Hospital Patiala and founder of her own private clinic, she brings world-class skin care to the city.
